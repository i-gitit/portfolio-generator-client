About

The portfolio generator is a platform where you can sign up and create your portfolios using interactive elements and tools. The web portfolio will serve as an organized, visual collection of your skills, achievements, projects, and efforts that will demonstrate your accomplishments.

You can share your public portfolio link using which people can view your qualifications and projects, also any services that you provide. The platform also provides the feature of contact cards which is the web version of old days business cards.
